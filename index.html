About Me

So, who am I?



Back in 2001, I was a fresh-faced teenager looking to make my mark in the world. I wanted to make a comic book, but those were so old-hat (even then), and I wanted to do something innovative, so I teamed up with my best friend who was also an aspiring web developer and we made a web comic. It was a fantastic time, and I got much more exposure online than I would have making physical zines.

Later, in 2014, my friend called me again asking if I wanted to help him design a mobile game. I said yes. A month-and-a-half later came Blobfish Evolution, which went on to be played by over 1.5 million people, and featured by major YouTubers like PewDiePie and Matthias.

That experience sealed the deal for me, and shortly thereafter I went to Code Fellows and learned how to make beautiful and compelling online experiences.
